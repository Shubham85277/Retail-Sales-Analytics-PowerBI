# Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze retail sales, profitability, customer trends, and product performance using the Global Superstore dataset.

The dashboard enables business users to monitor key performance indicators, identify top-performing products, evaluate regional profitability, and analyze sales trends over time.

## Dataset

* Dataset: Global Superstore
* Records Analyzed: 51,290+
* Unique Orders: 25,000+
* Customers: 2,000+
* Time Period: 2011–2014

## Key Metrics

* Total Sales: 12.64M
* Total Profit: 1.47M
* Profit Margin: 11.61%
* Total Orders: 25K+
* Total Customers: 2K+

## Dashboard Features

* Executive KPI Monitoring
* Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
* Category-wise Sales Performance
* Region-wise Profit Analysis
* Top 5 Products by Sales
* Interactive Slicers (Year, Region, Category)

## Business Insights

* Generated over 12.64M in total sales during the analysis period.
* Achieved a profit margin of 11.61% across all transactions.
* Processed more than 25K customer orders.
* Served over 2K customers across multiple regions.
* Identified top-performing product categories and products contributing significantly to revenue.
* Regional profitability analysis highlighted differences in business performance across markets.
